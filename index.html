Instructions

  1. The game will pick a random number, and you guess the number by entering the number in the box, and press enter to submit your guess.
  2. You have upto five chance to guess the number correctly.
  3. The game will let you know if you guess is higher or lower to the random number
  4. You win if you guess the number correctly within 5 guesses.
